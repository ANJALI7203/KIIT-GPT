FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries. For detailed information, ask KIIT-GPT.

How do I apply to KIIT? ▾
Visit kiit.ac.in, register online, fill the application form, and appear for KIITEE (or submit your JEE/CLAT/NEET score as applicable). Counselling is conducted online after results are declared.
What is the minimum attendance requirement? ▾
75% attendance is mandatory in every subject per semester to be eligible for end-semester examinations. Students between 65–75% can apply for condonation. Below 65% results in de-registration.
What is the B.Tech fee structure at KIIT? ▾
B.Tech tuition fee at KIIT is approximately ₹17–19 Lakhs for the full 4-year program (₹4.25–4.75 Lakhs per year). Hostel charges are separate (approx. ₹1.2–1.8 Lakhs/year). Merit-based scholarships are available.
Is hostel accommodation mandatory for first-year students? ▾
Yes, hostel accommodation is generally compulsory for outstation first-year students at KIIT. Both boys' and girls' hostels are available on campus with dining, Wi-Fi, reading rooms, and security.
What entrance exams does KIIT accept? ▾
KIIT accepts: KIITEE (its own entrance exam) for B.Tech, JEE Main for B.Tech, NEET for MBBS/BDS, KLSAT/CLAT for law, CAT/MAT/XAT/CMAT for MBA, and GATE for M.Tech.
How do I access the SAP portal? ▾
The SAP portal is accessible at the KIIT student portal website. Login credentials are provided after admission. It manages attendance, exam schedules, results, fees, and academic records.
