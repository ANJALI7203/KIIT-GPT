Attendance Policy
A minimum of 75% attendance is mandatory in each subject per semester to be eligible to appear in end-semester examinations. Students with less than 75% but above 65% may apply for a condonation of shortage on valid medical/personal grounds. Below 65% results in de-registration for that course.
Admissions

How to Join KIIT

Admission to most programs is through KIITEE — KIIT's own entrance exam. Other national exams are also accepted.

1
Register & Apply Online
Visit kiit.ac.in and fill the online application form. Upload required documents: 10th & 12th marksheets, photo, signature.
2
Appear in KIITEE
KIITEE (KIIT Entrance Examination) is conducted online in multiple sessions. For B.Tech, JEE scores are also accepted. For MBA: CAT/MAT/XAT. For Law: KLSAT/CLAT.
3
Merit List & Counselling
Shortlisted candidates are called for counselling (online/offline) where they choose their preferred program and branch based on merit rank.
4
Fee Payment & Admission
Pay the admission fee online to confirm your seat. Complete document verification. Receive your student ID and report to campus.

Key Eligibility Criteria

B.Tech
60% aggregate in 10+2 with PCM. Valid KIITEE or JEE (Main) score.
MBA
50% aggregate in any Bachelor's degree. Valid CAT / MAT / XAT / CMAT / GMAT / ATMA score.
Law (BA LLB)
50% in 10+2 from recognised board. KLSAT or CLAT score. Age below 20 years (22 for SC/ST).
MBBS / BDS
50% in PCB in 10+2. Qualifying NEET UG score is mandatory.
M.Tech
B.Tech / BE in relevant discipline. GATE score accepted. KIITEE also conducted for M.Tech.
