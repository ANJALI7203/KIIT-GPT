Career Development

How KIIT Prepares You

KIIT's placement success isn't accidental — it's the result of systematic industry-aligned training and career support.

🎯
Kareer School
Dedicated career centre providing guidance on internships, job placements, and higher education. Mock interviews, aptitude training, and soft skills workshops.
🤝
Industry Tie-Ups
Consistent recruiter engagement with 700+ companies through Centers of Excellence, sponsored labs, and long-term MoU partnerships for internships and hiring.
🌐
Alumni Network
A strong alumni network provides mentorship, guest lectures, and referral opportunities. Alumni at top global companies actively refer and recruit KIIT graduates.
🏆
Certification Programs
Industry certifications from Microsoft, AWS, Google, Oracle, and others integrated into the curriculum to boost employability.
💡
KIIT-TBI Incubator
Students with entrepreneurial ambitions can leverage KIIT-TBI — India's leading incubator — for funding, mentorship, and launchpad support.
📱
SAP Portal
Students access placement notifications, application portals, schedule management, and recruiter communication through the centralized SAP portal.
